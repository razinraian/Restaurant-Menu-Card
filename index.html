Chilli Prawn Skewers

380 Taka

Juicy tiger prawns marinated in smoky paprika, fresh lime juice, and fiery bird's eye chilli, then grilled to perfection on aromatic rosewood skewers. Served with a cooling yogurt dip and a wedge of lemon. Each skewer is carefully prepared by our chef using only the freshest ingredients sourced daily from local markets. The prawns are soaked overnight in our secret spice blend to ensure maximum flavour in every bite. A perfect starter for spice lovers!

Crispy Halloumi Bites

290 Taka

Golden-fried halloumi cubes coated in a light crispy batter, served with a tangy sweet chilli dipping sauce and fresh micro-herbs on a rustic slate board. Our halloumi is imported directly from Cyprus and fried fresh to order. Each bite delivers a satisfying crunch on the outside with a warm, soft, and slightly salty cheese centre. Topped with a squeeze of lemon and a sprinkle of smoked paprika for extra depth of flavour. A favourite among our vegetarian guests and cheese lovers alike!
